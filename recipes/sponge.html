Sponge Cake

Sponge cake is a light and airy cake thatperfect for layering with cream and fruit.

Ingredients

  • 300g flour
  • 200gg sugar
  • 150g Vegan cream cheese
  • 200ml double plant cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 200ml plant milk
  • 100ml vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp fruit jam (optional)

Image source: Andy Li, Wikimedia Commons

Method

  1. Mix flour and sugar until well combined.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients with the wet ingredients, folding gently.
  3. Pour into a greased and lined cake tin.
  4. Bake at 180Â°C for 25-30 minutes until a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Repeat previous steps for a double layered cake.
  6. Allow to cool completely before slicing and layering with whipped cream and fruit.
  7. Whip the vegan cream cheese and double plant cream together until smooth.
  8. Spread the cream mixture and jam between the layers of sponge and on top.
