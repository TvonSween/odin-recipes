Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert that combines layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers with a rich and creamy mascarpone mixture, all dusted with cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • 300g Silken Tofu
  • 200g plain biscuits (rich tea, marieta, etc), sponge or ladyfingers
  • 150g Vegan cream cheese
  • 50g sugar
  • 1 cup Strong Coffee, cooled
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Dark chocolate, chopped

Method

  1. Blend silken tofu with sugar until smooth.
  2. Mix in vegan cream cheese.
  3. Soak biscuits in coffee and layer with the cream mixture.
  4. Chill for at least 4 hours before serving.
  5. Dust with cocoa powder and sprinkle with chopped dark chocolate before serving.
